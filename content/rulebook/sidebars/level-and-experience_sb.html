TL;DR — Leveling & Advancement

▸
  • Tracks & XP
    There are 3 Tracks: Combat, Social, and Exploration.
    You gain XP in each Track separately — not all experience is equal.
  • Leveling Up
    Every 5 XP in a Track grants a new Level.
    Each Level lets you pick a new Advancement from that Track’s list.
  • Growth is Asymmetric
    Characters don’t level evenly. You might be Level 4 in Exploration, but only Level 1 in Social.
  • Respec & Retraining
    With GM approval, you can retrain one Advancement after major events — like trauma, study, or rebirth.
